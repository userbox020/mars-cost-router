# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone.
Participate with empathy, respect differing viewpoints, give constructive
feedback, and avoid harassment, threats, discrimination, or publishing another
person's private information.

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation
when conduct is inappropriate, threatening, abusive, or harmful. Report conduct
concerns privately through the repository maintainers. Reports will be reviewed
promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing
the project.
